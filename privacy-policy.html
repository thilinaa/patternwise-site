Privacy Policy

Last updated: 4 July 2026

The short version. Patternwise is a private, local-first wellness journal. Your health data lives only on your device, encrypted. There is no account, and we do not collect, receive, store, sell, or share it. The only thing that ever leaves your phone is an optional, coarse weather lookup — details below.

1. Who this applies to

This policy covers the Patternwise iOS app. Patternwise is provided by an independent developer (contact below). Because the app has no backend server and no account system, there is no central place where your data is gathered — it stays with you.

2. What is stored on your device

Everything you put into Patternwise is stored locally in an encrypted database on your iPhone:

  • What you log — symptoms and their severity, food and drinks, lifestyle factors (sleep, stress, exercise, and any custom ones), notes, and the profiles of the people you track.
  • Auto-captured context — weather/pressure and, if you allow it, last night's sleep.
  • Settings — theme, reminders, and similar preferences.

We have no access to any of this. It never touches our servers, because we don't operate any.

3. What we do NOT collect

Patternwise contains no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking SDKs. We do not collect your name, email, contacts, device identifiers, usage analytics, or crash logs. We cannot see your logs, and we cannot identify you.

4. The one exception: weather & approximate location

If you enable weather features, the app asks a third-party weather service (Open-Meteo) for local weather and barometric pressure. To do that it sends an approximate, coarse location — a low-precision area, not your exact position or address. This is used only to fetch the weather for that request; we do not store it and it is not linked to your identity or used to track you. You can leave weather off, and no location is ever sent.

5. Apple Health (HealthKit)

If you grant permission, Patternwise reads selected data — such as last night's sleep — from Apple Health to save you typing. This access is read-only: we never write to Apple Health, and the data stays on your device. It is never transmitted anywhere.

6. On-device AI

Optional AI features (for example, a plain-language recap of what you've logged, or a neutral summary for a doctor's export) run entirely on your device using Apple's on-device models. Your entries are not sent to any server — ours or anyone else's — to power these features. The AI only describes what you logged; it does not diagnose or predict.

7. Notifications

Optional reminders are scheduled locally on your device. No reminder content is sent to a server.

9. How your data is protected

Your on-device database is encrypted at rest. The encryption key is held in the device's secure keychain, so your entries are protected by your iPhone's own security. Deleting the app removes its data from your device.

10. Data retention & your control

Your data stays on your device until you remove it. You are in control: you can edit or delete any entry inside the app, archive or delete conditions and factors, and delete the app to erase everything. Because we never receive your data, there is nothing for us to delete on your behalf.

11. Sharing & selling

We do not sell your data, and we do not share it — we never have it. If you choose to export a CSV or PDF and send it to someone (for example, your doctor), that sharing is entirely under your control and happens through the apps you pick.

12. Purchases (future)

Patternwise is currently free. If optional paid features are added in a future version, purchases will be handled by Apple's App Store together with a payments provider (RevenueCat) that processes anonymous purchase and subscription events to unlock features on your device. No health data is ever included. This policy will be updated before any such feature ships.

13. Children

Patternwise is not directed to children under 13. It is a personal wellness journal intended for adults, who may also keep notes on behalf of someone they care for.

14. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal data held about you (for example, under the GDPR or CCPA). Because Patternwise keeps your data only on your device and we hold nothing about you, you can exercise all of these directly in the app. If you have any questions, contact us.

15. Not medical advice

Patternwise is a wellness journal, not a medical device. It does not diagnose, treat, or predict any condition.

16. Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update this page and the "Last updated" date at the top. Material changes will be reflected here before they take effect.
