Why Patternwise

Several conditions, together

Most trackers follow one thing. Patternwise holds migraine, reflux and your own conditions side by side — so cross-connections can surface.

Logged in seconds

Pick what and how intense — done. Food, sleep, stress and custom factors take a tap. Auto-capture handles weather and last night's sleep.

Private by design

Everything is stored encrypted on your device. There's no account and no server. Optional AI summaries run entirely on-device.

Gentle, honest insights

A calendar and frequency view, plus a look-back at what you logged around an entry — for context, never as a cause. No predictions.

For you, or someone you care for

Keep separate private profiles for more than one person on the same phone.

Yours to keep

Export a clean CSV or a tidy PDF summary to keep, or to share with your doctor.

Patternwise is a wellness journal, not a medical device. It does not diagnose, treat, or predict any condition. For anything health-related, talk with your doctor.
