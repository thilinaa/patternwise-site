Support

Patternwise is a calm, private journal for noticing your own health patterns — migraine, reflux, sleep, food and the weather around you, tracked together on your phone.

Need help, found a bug, or have an idea? Email support.patternwise@gmail.com. We read every message and usually reply within a couple of days.

What Patternwise does

• Log symptoms in seconds, with optional detail
• Track food, drinks & lifestyle factors
• Track several conditions together
• Separate private profiles per person
• Calendar & frequency insights
• Look-back: context around each entry
• Auto-capture weather, pressure & sleep
• Export a CSV or PDF for your doctor

Frequently asked

Do I need an account?
No. Patternwise has no account and no sign-in. You just open the app and start. Everything stays on your device.
Where is my data stored? Is it private?
In an encrypted database on your phone. Nothing is uploaded to a server — there is no server. There are no ads and no trackers. See the Privacy Policy for details.
How do I log something quickly?
On the Today screen, tap Symptom, Food, or Lifestyle, pick what and how intense, and save. You can also type a sentence like "bad headache after late coffee" into the quick box and confirm the entries it suggests.
How do I edit or delete an entry?
Open History (or "See all" on Today) and press and hold any entry to edit or delete it.
How do I export my data?
Settings → Export data. Choose a person, what to include, and a date range, then create a CSV (a spreadsheet of everything) or a PDF summary to keep or share with a clinician.
Can I track more than one person?
Yes. Add profiles in Settings → People, and switch between them from the avatar on the Today screen. Each person keeps their own conditions, logs, and insights, privately.
What are the "AI features"? Are they private?
Optional summaries (a recap of your logs, and a neutral doctor's-export summary) run entirely on your device using Apple Intelligence. Nothing is sent to a server. They require a device with Apple Intelligence enabled; without it, everything else in the app still works.
Does it use my location?
Only if you enable weather. It then sends a coarse, approximate area (not your exact location) to a weather service to fetch local conditions and pressure, and keeps nothing. You can leave weather off.
Does it read Apple Health?
Only with your permission, and only to read (for example) last night's sleep so you don't have to type it. Patternwise never writes to Apple Health.
Is my data backed up? What if I delete the app?
In this version, data lives only on your device — there is no cloud backup, which is what keeps it fully private. Deleting the app erases its data. To move to a new phone, export a CSV first. (Optional encrypted sync is planned for a future version.)
Does it work offline?
Yes. Everything except the optional weather lookup works with no connection.
Is Patternwise a medical app?
No. Patternwise is a wellness journal. It does not diagnose, treat, or predict any condition, and it is not a medical device. For anything health-related, talk with your doctor.
How much does it cost?
Patternwise is free. If optional paid features are added later, they will be clearly marked and you'll be able to choose.
How do I report a bug or request a feature?
Email support.patternwise@gmail.com with what happened (and your iPhone model / iOS version if it's a bug). It really helps.
